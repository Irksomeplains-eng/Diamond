Game Modes: 2v2, 3v3, 4v4, 5v5

Rules

  • Once The Tournament Starts, You Will Recieve A Tournament (Role).
  • Be Sure to Be In A VC 15 Min Prior To The Tournament.
  • MOSS Must Be Running During Games Or Your Team Will Be Removed Unless Using Alternative Proof.

    • Fail To Provide Any Of These Options, Your Team Will Be DQed Without Further Review.
    • If Moss Corrupts, You Must Provide Proof Of Files Being Corrupted And A PC Check Will Be Requested. If Denied, Team Will Be Auto DQed.
    • MUST HAVE REASONABLE SUSPICION TO ASK FOR PROOF OF CHEATING.
  • Games are run by staff and streamed. Be ready when called.
  • Missing players = removal and possible ban from future events.
  • No subs allowed. Duo only.
  • If a PC check is requested, you must do it or get removed and banned.
  • If asked for a monitor cam by host, you must do it.
  • Teammates Must Be Present In Discord To Play.
  • Top matches will be streamed on Twitch.
  • Matches are first to 5. Finals are also first to 5.
  • You Must Follow itzd1amond On Twitch Or You’ll Be removed.
